Economy

Phoenix has a very strong economy, ranked #10 out of the 50 states. They excel in business environment's and economic growth. The job's are well paid and a high growth in job's. It's GDP is $326.446 Billion. The increasingly widespread use of air conditioning resulted in massive population and business growth in the state, including in the state capital, Phoenix, increasing it's sales in economy massively.

Cost of Living

Phoenix's living costs are one of the best in all of Arizona. The housing is just a bit above average in cost compared to other cities, but the grocery cost is surprisingly cheaper than the rest of the cities by more than 5%.
